About Zita Lerwick

"The objective of my business is to provide a high quality, professionally decorated, custom cake and personal service for customers. My cakes not only look wonderful but also taste wonderful. I make edible art. My talent comes from my love of baking at an early age. As a teen I would bake for friends and family. I hold a BS in Marketing and a BA in Business from DeSales University."
-Zita Lerwick

Growing up in Stow, Ohio, cake designer Aita Lerwick can't remember a time when she wasn't baking, following a family tradition that stretches back more than 100 years. Whenever she wasn’t in the kitchen, Zita was creating in other ways, making award winning art pieces.

Now living in the bright state of Florida Zita's passion for baking continues to flourish. A scientist by profession, Zita has the soul of an artist and now she combines both the art and science of baking to bring delicious cakes. All lovingly hand crafted using the finest ingredients and mouth-watering fillings and flavours.

Zita's cakes are works of edible art – she never creates a cake she wouldn't want a second slice of. You can guarantee that your guests will be awed by her exquisite and contemporary cake designs. Order wedding, birthday and celebration cakes from Zita to make your special occasion perfect.
